Japan 2019

In the fall of 2019, we will be traveling to Japan! We will be spending two weeks taking in the scenery, and learning about the Japanese culture. Peak fall-colors can be seen in the southern region of Honshu, including the Tokyo area, in early November. Our travels take us through Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, Nara, and finally Hakone.

Tokyo - 東京

We will arrive at Narita Airport, over 24 hours later the next day, after takeoff from JFK. Here's some information about what we'll do in Tokyo!

Kyoto - 京都

Kyoto was one of the surviving cities after World War II, with no bombings ever commenced on the city. It is believed the Allied forces considered the city to be too historic, deciding to refrain from attacking. Kyoto is home to traditional Kaeseki, dazzling Geisha, and ancient temples and shrines.
